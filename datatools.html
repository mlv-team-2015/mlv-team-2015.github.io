Brainstorm about why you are investigating this topic.

Are you interested in creating a representation of what is already known?

Is the goal to generate new knowledge via use of a data tool?

Do you have the resources to collect your own data?

What do you know about this topic, and expect to see in the data?

What data are available?

How are the data organized?

How will you represent the values?

What is your message?

Design decisions should enhance the impact of your message

For example:

  • What scales are the axes?
  • Are certain values that serve as natural break points?
  • How are the values grouped? What groupings are relevant?
  • Are the units alienating or understandable to the intended audience?
  • What is the emotional impact of your color choices?
  • Are the labels accurate and visible?
